BaristaLang

By Simmon Quan

About the language:

BaristaLang is a python based scripting language that aims to emulate a conversation a customer would have with a barista at a cafe. The goal of baristaLang is to have an easy to learn, human-speech like syntax that will hopefully get beginners to start coding.

Features ~ BaristaLang can interpret:

  • user provided math equations with operator precedence
  • if statements
  • print statements
  • FizzBuzz
  • prompts for websites
  • prompts for ASCII art
Example:
coffee = 10 + 20 matcha = 60 - 30 donut = 100 / 5 Can you show me latte art of my name DABISH? Can I take a look at your menu? orangeDrink If coffee is not in stock, can I get donut? If donut is not in stock, can I get orangeDrink? If milkShake is not in stock, can I get coffee? donut = (6 + 2) * (8 + 9) coffee = (5 - 2) / (3 - 0) I would like to order(donut) I would like to order(coffee) Does your cafe sell any fizzy drinks?
